The Land of Ériu

A civilization divided among the five warring kingdoms of men.

An island populated by ancient creatures and potent mysteries.

People of Ériu

Men

The kingdoms of men — Ulaid, Connacht, Leinster, Munster, and Mide — and their gods are recent arrivals to Ériu, and have yet to eject the previous inhabitants. Compared to the other races, men breed, live, and die at a startling speed.

Sidhe

No one knows how or when the Fair Folk came to Ériu. They prefer to live hidden from the eyes of men, but have been known to play tricks or work charms on mortals — some harmless, some less so. The sidhe are roughly divided into the Seelie and Unseelie Courts, but the distinction is often lost on outsiders.

Fir Bolg

A monstrous race led by their king Eochaid, the Fir Bolg were driven from Ériu by the armies of men and the Tuatha Dé Danann. Some remained, however, and fled into the dark places of the land. The new king Sreng plots their return to power over their ancient homeland.

Fomoire

The first rulers of Ériu, the Fomoire are said to be older than the land itself. Hideous giants, great in strength and skilled in dark, ancient magic, the Fomoire rival the Tuatha Dé themselves. Fortunately few of the Fomoire remain in Ériu.

Tuatha Dé Danann

The Tuatha Dé, the gods of men, have rarely been seen since they displaced the Fir Bolg and vanquished the Fomoire.

Dynamic Quest System

Choose your path:

  • Raise an army to destroy your enemies
  • Gain fame and wealth so that your name will live in legend
  • Lift an ancient curse from your family line
  • Travel to a holy site to work a great spell

Use your fame and wealth to recruit NPCs to your cause.

As your power and reputation grow, unlock new quests to help you toward your main goal.

Chronicle Your Deeds

Though the land changes for every new game, your past deeds live on in rumor, song, and legend.

Share the tale of your adventures through the Online Chronicles.
